About The CROW

The Campus Research & Observational Writings (CROW) at UWB is an ongoing collaborative process that seeks to highlight the superior research conducted by UWB students, and to provide space for comparing, analyzing, and generally thinking about the ways research and knowledge production intersect. Our mission is to publish research on a broad range of topics spanning all disciplines, levels of analysis, and national contexts. With collaboration from the Writing and Communication Center, The CROW offers an opportunity for students to publish their work, as well as hone their writing skills and emerge as more effective writers.
